Moon — 6 iterations on Realistic Full (#11)

6 variations of moon-realistic-full — 3 small tonal/lighting changes keeping the same lunar character, 3 major variations showing what the SVG filter stack does for genuinely different moon types (icy, volcanic, ancient-cratered).

Locked from prior rounds: Google Noto Emoji · Twitter Twemoji · Etched (17) · Holographic (16) · Watercolor (14) · Constellation (19) · Realistic Full Illuminated (11). Plus the original 10 procedurals from batch 1. Adding whichever of these 6 you like to the set.

Small variations tonal / lighting

21. Selene Classic small

moon-realistic-selene
The baseline, slightly refined. Warm-cream base, blue atmospheric halo, standard upper-left lighting. Earth's moon.

22. Pale Silver small

moon-realistic-silver
Lighter, more reflective base (almost white). Glossier specular hotspot. Fewer crater features. Cool blue atmospheric halo. Reads "young / pristine."

23. Warm Cream small

moon-realistic-warm
Warmer base (cream / sandy tan). Brown-toned maria + craters instead of grey. Golden specular hotspot. Warm honey-amber halo. Reads "golden hour."

Major variations different worlds entirely

24. Ancient Cratered major

moon-realistic-ancient
Heavy cratering (~30+ visible), dusty/oxidized brown tones, deeper maria, weaker specular. Reads "old, weathered, Mercury-like."

25. Icy (Europa-style) major

moon-realistic-icy
White-blue icy surface, network of dark cracks (linea) crisscrossing the surface, very few craters (young ice resurfacing), strong cold specular, blue atmospheric halo.

26. Volcanic (Io-style) major

moon-realistic-volcanic
Yellow-orange sulfurous surface, dark caldera patches, 3 active red-orange volcanic glow spots, warm orange atmospheric halo. Reads "alive, dangerous, geologically active."
