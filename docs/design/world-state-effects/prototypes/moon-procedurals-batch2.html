Realistic — SVG filter shading 3 moons

Each uses a layered filter stack: feDiffuseLighting with a distant light source for spherical surface shading, feSpecularLighting with a point light for highlight, feTurbulence + feColorMatrix for surface texture. Maria placed at real lunar coordinates (Imbrium, Serenitatis, Tranquillitatis, Crisium, Fecunditatis, Nubium). Discrete craters have bright sun-facing rims + dark shadow rims.
11. Realistic Full Illuminated
moon-realistic-full
Spherical shading via radial gradient + feDiffuseLighting bevel. feSpecularLighting hotspot at upper-left. Real maria + Tycho ray system. Atmospheric edge halo.
12. Realistic Sidelit (Half Moon)
moon-realistic-sidelit
Strong directional light from left → dramatic terminator. Only the lit half shows surface features. Subtle earthshine (blue cast) on the dark side — light bouncing off Earth.
13. Realistic Blood Moon (Eclipse)
moon-realistic-eclipse
Reddish-orange gradient from atmospheric refraction during lunar eclipse. Umbral shadow on edge, soft red corona around. Maria + craters still visible through the red. Star field behind.
