Standard weather

Clear

weather-clear
Subtle drifting wisps. The "nothing's happening but the sky's alive" baseline.

Cloudy

weather-cloudy
Three parallax cloud layers at different speeds. Soft white blobs.

Rain

weather-rain
Falling diagonal streaks. Density configurable (light → heavy).

Thunderstorm

weather-thunderstorm
Heavy rain + occasional lightning flash (~every 4s).

Snow

weather-snow
Drifting flakes with sway. Slower vertical velocity than rain.

Fog

weather-fog
Large translucent grey blobs drifting horizontally. Multiple layers.

Severe / dramatic

Tornado

weather-tornado
Rotating funnel + debris particles spiraling around it. Dark sky.

Ashfall

weather-ashfall
Grey flakes drifting down slowly. Reddish-tinted sky from distant fire.

Celestial events

Meteor Shower

celestial-meteor-shower
Bright streaks with glowing trails falling diagonally. Star field behind.

Aurora

celestial-aurora
Rippling green-violet curtains shimmering across the sky. Stars behind.
